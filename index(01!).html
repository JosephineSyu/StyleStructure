CLEMENTINE (CONT'D)

What if you stay this time?

JOEL

I walked out the door. There's no more memory.

CLEMENTINE

Come back and make up a good-bye at least.

Let's pretend we had one.

CLEMENTINE (CONT'D)

I love you.
